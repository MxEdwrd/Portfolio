About Max

My name is Max Edward. I am currently majoring in Digital Making and Fabrication at the Univerity of Advancing Technologies in Tempe, Arizona.

Max Edward

Hello! My name is Max Edward. I was born in Glendale, Arizona and have lived in Phoenix, Arizona my entire life. I am currently majoring in Digital Making and Fabrication at the University of Advancing Technology. The core of my interests includes 3D Printing and Modeling. I am also an amateur photographer, motorcycle mechanic, and love to go hiking and camping.

How I got into 3D printing

My interest in 3D printing started when I was 16 years old. I started with a cheap 3D printer I purchased on a whim after remembering that I heard about 3D printing many years prior and being interested. After getting the machine and realizing all the possible things I could create with it, I began my journey in 3D printing and modeling.

My future in the 3D build world

I hope that with being at UAT, I can learn more and gain real-world experience in the 3D build scene. I still have a long way to go as I have only just started my journey. I hope to find a job in the 3D build sector of engineering - preferably with a company that does 3D printer design or something alike. As I spend more time learning and gain more experience, I hope to become a better 3D builder and take my skills to the stars!
